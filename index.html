About the Cause

Rural India Is Suffering.

Citizens living in rural and slum areas are the ones who bear the blunt force of the toxins that are released into the air during the winter months. And when you realize that these are the very same citizens who do not have access to facilities like air purifiers and masks, you see that the people who do burst crackers aren't paying the price: the lower classes of society are.
Every year, they lose loved ones to the same diseases that we are prone to: heart diseases, lung cancer, respiratory infections, and so many more. And they have no way of getting out of this ugly cycle with the financial constraints on them. And so, Project Saans was conceptualized with a simple, profound idea: everyone deserves clean air.

About the Cause

Change Is Possible. Change is Essential.

Last year, we were able to raise enough funds to supply hundreds of respiratory masks for free in rural areas, right before the brute force of the pollution hit New Delhi. At Project Saans, we believe that not only is more change possible, it's needed. That's our mission. To guarantee the basic right of clean air to every citizen of India.
